    From a young age, I’ve always been a curious and charismatic explorer, with an thirst for knowledge. Although I never knew exactly what I wanted to do, one thing was always clear: my future was in IT. The path to where I am now hasn’t been a straight line, but an exciting journey filled with learning experiences.

    During my studies in Software Development, I found myself in a world where creativity and logic meet. This is where I discovered my passion for building digital solutions. But that’s just the beginning. Throughout my journey so far, I’ve gained a wide range of IT-related experiences, from working as an IT technician to developing websites and tackling challenges that sharpened my technical and problem-solving skills.

    In addition to my technical background, I took on a new challenge from December to June by diving into the world of debating. I participated in several debate tournaments, using the structured and logical thinking I developed as a coder to craft strong arguments and deliver them effectively. One of my proudest moments was achieving 3rd place at a prestigious tournament hosted by the Amsterdam City Council, an experience that sharpened both my public speaking and critical thinking skills.

    Beyond debating and coding, I am constantly seeking ways to further develop myself, both personally and professionally. Whether I’m attending seminars and workshops or learning new programming languages and tools, I am always striving to expand my knowledge. While I’m still exploring my true passion within the IT world, I know one thing for sure: I will never stop growing. Every experience, challenge, and success brings me one step closer. Whether it’s writing code or developing ideas, I believe that the pursuit of constant growth and development is at the core of who I am and what I do.
